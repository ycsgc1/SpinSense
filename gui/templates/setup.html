Welcome to SpinSense

A few quick steps to calibrate your turntable. You can change any of this later in Settings.

mic

Pick your microphone

Choose the input device receiving audio from your turntable. If you're not sure, try "System default" first — you can change it later.

graphic_eq

Calibrate your threshold

Drop the needle on a record but don't play music yet. Watch where the bar peaks — that's your noise floor. Nudge the slider just above it.

Volume threshold 0.0000

If the bar isn't moving, the engine isn't getting any audio yet — check your mic selection.

router

Connect to Home Assistant

Enter your MQTT broker's address and credentials. SpinSense publishes track info to MQTT for Home Assistant to pick up. Don't have a broker? Skip this step.

You're all set

Saving will apply your settings to the running engine. You can fine-tune anything in Settings later.
